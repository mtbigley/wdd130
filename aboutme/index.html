I'm an avid reader, a water sports enthusiast, an astronomy geek, a gamer, an archery geek, a dabbler in poetry, and many other things.

I married the love of my life, Savannah, 2 years ago, and have one four-month-old daughter who grows bigger, louder and happier each day. In the past I would take any job that paid the bills, but I hope to go into machine-learning and apply what I learn to the Aerospace industry. What I love most about BYUI is how supportive it is. If you need help, advice or even just friends, the faculty and church have thought of everything. I'm currently pursuing a Bachelor's in computer science, with a minor in Mechanical Engineering.
